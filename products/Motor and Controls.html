Motor Descriptions

AC motors manufactured by Marathon, Baldor, Teco, Reliance, and Tatung

Premium Efficiency

Manufactured by Baldor, Teco, Reliance, and Marathon. These premium efficiency designs are available in both TEFC and Open Drip proof construction. All 3-phase motors are inventory ready per NEMA Standard MG1, Part 31.4.4.2. This means the motors in 230v and 460v meet NEMA’s corona inception voltage requirements and can withstand peak voltages up to 1600v. Motors having premium efficiency are also available in single phase.

Brake Motors

Manufactured by Baldor, Teco, Reliance, and Marathon. Brake motors may be used on machine tools, conveyors, door operators, speed reducers, hoists, or any application requiring quick stops and positive holds.

HVAC

Manufactured by Baldor, Marathon, Reliance, Teco ranging in size from 1/4 to 75HP and include heavy duty industrial designs as well as designs for commercial applications. These applications include ventilators, direct drive and belted fans, exhaust fans, unit heaters, air conditioning units, and commercial refrigeration condensers. Also included are motors designed especially for use with variable torque electronic controls that are used to conserve electricity.

Metric

Manufactured by Baldor, Marathon, Reliance, Cemp, and ABB. Metric dimension motors are designed in IEC frames from D63 through D200L. The motors are available in 50Hz or 60 Hz. Motor construction meets IP54 and includes motors with B3 base mounting, B5 Flange mounting, or B14 Face mounting. Stock motors range in size from .18kW to 30 kW. Typical applications for this product include pumps, fans, conveyors, machine tools, gear reducers and any other jobs that requires IEC frames.

Brake Motors

Manufactured by Baldor, Teco, Reliance, and Marathon. Brake motors may be used on machine tools, conveyors, door operators, speed reducers, hoists, or any application requiring quick stops and positive holds.

Pump

Manufactured by Baldor, Marathon, North American, and Reliance. Pump motors include single or three phase jet pump motors, square flange motors, close-coupled pump motors, and P-base vertical solid shaft pump motors. The motors are stocked in TEFC, open drip proof, and many explosion proof ratings. Applications for these motors include a wide range, from swimming pool pumps to medium and high thrust in-line pumps used in water treatment plants. Horse powers range in size from 1/3 through 75 HP.

ATEX (Flameproof)

Manufactured by ABB, Cemp, Marathon. Available in either Zone 1 or Zone 2 classifications. Available in 1–100HP. These cast iron motors meet automotive requirements for sound power levels.

Commercial Motors

Manufactured by Baldor, GE, and Marathon. This definite purpose product line is designed for certain applications requiring NEMA 48 and 56 motors rated between 1/4 and 1 HP, in both single and three phase ratings. Typical applications include jet pump, heating & cooling, ventilation, air conditioning, blowers, direct drive, condenser, and pedestal fans.

Explosion Proof

Manufactured by Baldor, Marathon, North American, Reliance, Tatung, and Teco. Explosion proof motors are used in applications where hazardous fumes or dust may be present. The motors are available in both single and three phase designs, from 1/4 to 300 HP. Can include standard foot mounted, C-face with base, and C-face less base, 60 Hz, jet pump motors, and inverter drive motors.

General Purpose

Manufactured by Baldor, Marathon, Reliance, Tatung, and Teco, North American. General purpose motors are available in open drop proof and TEFC construction in both single and three phase. Sizes from 1/12 to 1500 HP are available. These general purpose motors are for use in a variety of applications such as compressors, pumps, fans, conveyors, material handling, machine tools, and many others. Also available are stock motors which are direct replacements for the motors built since 1952. NEMA frames.

Vertical Pump Motor

Manufactured by North American, Reliance, Tatung. Stocking VHS high thrust motors from 5HP to 250 HP in 4 and 6 pole TEFC 3/60/460 volts. Custom VSS motors in various enclosures (WPI, WPII, TEFC, TEAAC, TEWAC) in voltages 460, 4160, 6600, 13.8 Kv in either 50 or 60 Hz. Motors designed for your individual application.

Medium Voltage Motors

Manufactured by Teco, Marathon, Baldor, Reliance, GE. Horizontal and vertical medium/high voltage motors from 2300/4160 6600 and 13.8KV. Enclosures WPI, WPII, ODP, TEFC, TEAAC, TEWC. Motors can be built to IEC or NEMA standards.
